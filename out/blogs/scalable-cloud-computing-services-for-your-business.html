Types of Cloud Service Models 

Understanding which forms of cloud computing to adopt can help you make better business decisions. These solutions address multiple organizational needs under different cloud models. 

Cloud Deployment Models 

  • Public Cloud: Cost-effective and fully scalable solutions hosted on shared platforms. 
  • Private Cloud: Greater control and enhanced security in isolated environments. 
  • Hybrid Cloud: A mix of public and private clouds for maximum flexibility. 
  • Community Cloud: Shared among organizations with similar concerns or needs. 

What Are the Three Main Cloud Computing Service Models? 

  1. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) for scalable IT resources. 
  2. Platform as a Service (PaaS) for accelerated development cycles. 
  3. Software as a Service (SaaS) for enhanced operational productivity. 

These cloud delivery services are designed to empower businesses at every stage of their IT needs
